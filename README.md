## Playbook: Cold Email Outbound for Early-Stage B2B SaaS

Based on the research collected in this repository, I created a practical playbook focused on cold email outbound for early-stage B2B SaaS teams.

### Why this topic
Across the sources, a clear strategic disagreement emerged between content-first trust building and direct-response outbound. This playbook takes a position on that disagreement and translates the research into an actionable SOP.

### Playbook location
📄 `playbook/b2b-saas-email-playbook.md`

### Playbook outline
1. Scope & assumptions
2. Core philosophy: when cold email works
3. Cold email outbound SOP (step-by-step execution)
4. Where experts disagree
5. What I rejected and why
6. My original ideas
7. Weaknesses of this playbook
8. Who I would NOT recommend following and why
