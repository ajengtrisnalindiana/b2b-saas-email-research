### Post 1: Email Is Not Dead — It's the Most Important Channel Marketers Have

- Date: June 18, 2024
- Link: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/davegerhardt_email-marketing-teardown-best-practices-activity-7209140407333470210-yJys
- Topic: Email marketing importance, channel strategy, B2B email best practices
- Summary:
Dave argues that email is still the most important channel for B2B marketers, especially as organic reach on LinkedIn declines and AI-powered search eats away at organic traffic. While email faces challenges (lower open rates, tabs, tracking pixel blocking), it remains one of the few channels marketers can truly "own" to reach audiences directly.

- Key ideas:
Email is not dead — I'd argue it's the most important channel (still) that us marketers have
Organic reach on LinkedIn is declining — I have 166,000 followers but reach < 10% of that audience with each post
Organic search landscape is changing — Google serves AI-based answers in search vs. sending traffic back to your site
Tools like ChatGPT and Perplexity are eating away at traffic too
Challenges with email today: gone are the days of 50% open rates and double-digit click-through rates; the inbox has changed with tabs and promotions and blocking tracking pixels
But good ol' email still delivers if you know how to do it right — it's one of the few channels in marketing that you can truly "own" and reach your audience directly
Daniel J. Murphy (COO at Exit Five) added: 711 registrants for an Exit Five event and about 90% of them came from email

- Why this matters for B2B SaaS email / newsletter research:
Dave provides the strategic rationale for prioritizing email in an AI-disrupted marketing landscape. The specific data point — 90% of event registrations came from email — is a powerful case study for B2B SaaS companies debating channel investment. The observation about declining organic reach on LinkedIn (10% of 166K followers) explains why building an email list is critical even for creators with large social followings.

- Key quote: "Email is one of the few channels in marketing that you can truly 'own' and reach your audience directly when you want to."
