### Post : Does Outbound Still Work? — 13-Point Checklist

- Date: April 17, 2024
- Link: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/jasonmlemkin_does-outbound-still-work-its-funny-two-activity-7186823302806437888-nkCE
- Topic: Outbound email effectiveness, cold outreach strategy
- Summary:
Jason addresses the question of whether outbound still works in B2B SaaS.
He shares a 13-point checklist for making cold email actually effective,
emphasizing that email remains powerful when done correctly with strong value.

- Key ideas:
Email remains a magical thing — if the email is strong and the value is strong, it will get opened and read
Sending 20,000 poorly crafted emails does not count as "testing" outbound
Outbound is not the same as "fake personalization at scale"
Most founders have not actually tried real outbound yet
Many people who say outbound is dead have never done it well
Why this matters for B2B SaaS email / newsletter research:
Direct validation that email still works in B2B SaaS when executed correctly.
The 13-point checklist provides a practical audit framework for cold email
campaigns. The distinction between "real outbound" and "scaled fake personalization"
is critical for teams building email-driven growth strategies.

- Key quote: "Email remains a magical thing. If the email is strong, and importantly, the value strong — it will get opened and it will get read."
