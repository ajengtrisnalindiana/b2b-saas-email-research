### Post: Advanced B2B Positioning (Guest post by April Dunford)

- Date: April 2024
- Link: https://lnkd.in/gExJ62mS
- Topic: B2B SaaS positioning & differentiation
- Summary:
  Lenny shares an in-depth guest post by April Dunford on advanced B2B positioning.
  The post outlines four common positioning challenges teams face as product
  development becomes easier and competition for attention increases.

- Key ideas:
  1. Teams struggle to agree on what alternatives they are positioning against
  2. Product pessimism hides real strengths
  3. Differentiated value is often vague or poorly articulated
  4. Companies lack clarity on what they are actually positioning

- Why this matters for B2B SaaS email / newsletter research:
  Strong positioning is the foundation of effective email messaging.
  Without clear differentiation, newsletters and product emails struggle to
  capture attention or communicate value. This post highlights how narrative
  clarity directly impacts distribution and audience engagement.
