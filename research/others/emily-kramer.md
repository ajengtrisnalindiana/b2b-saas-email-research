## Summary
This newsletter explains how to structure growth marketing efforts, including prioritization, experimentation, and alignment across channels. It provides strategic context that can inform how newsletters and email campaigns fit into a broader B2B SaaS growth strategy.

## Why this matters
This resource helps explain how newsletters are used as part of a long-term growth system, not just a one-off content channel.
